Mapping Honky Tonk Tuesday

I don't remember when I discovered the Honky Tonk Tuesday archive, but ever since I've been determined to map it. One of my first questions was where does this music come from? This map shows the birthplace of the original artists, weighted by how many times their songs were played by the Honky Tonk Tuesday band.

Thanks to Honky Tonk Tuesday for providing the data, and to Chris Essig for sharing a great application template.
